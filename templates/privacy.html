Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our personal expense tracking service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly to us, such as your account details (name and email address) and the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic technical information, like your browser type, to keep the service running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the service: to store your expenses, generate summaries and reports, secure your account, and respond to your requests. We do not sell your personal or financial information, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely and is only accessible through your authenticated account. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third party providers for essential functions such as hosting and infrastructure. These providers only process data as needed to support the service and are expected to maintain appropriate safeguards. We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We are happy to help.
